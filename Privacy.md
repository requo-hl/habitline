# Privacy Policy for Habitline

**Last Updated: February 23, 2026**

Habitline ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Windows application.

### 1. Information We Collect
When you sign in using Google or Discord, we collect:
* **Email Address:** To identify your account and sync your habits.
* **Profile Information:** Your name and profile picture to personalize your experience.

### 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information solely to:
* Authenticate your identity via Supabase.
* Provide habit-tracking services across your devices.
* Communicate important updates regarding your account.

### 3. Data Storage and Security
Your data is securely stored using **Supabase** (powered by AWS/Google Cloud). We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share data with service providers (like Supabase) necessary to run the app.
