Privacy Policy for Habitline

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Habitline is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our Windows application.

1. Information Collection

We use Google and Discord to authenticate your identity. We collect your email address and basic profile name to create your account.

2. Use of Data

Your data is used solely to provide habit-tracking features and sync your progress via Supabase. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Security

All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption provided by Supabase and its infrastructure partners.
